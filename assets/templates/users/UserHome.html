Welcome to the Road Damage Detection System

This tool helps identify road damages such as cracks, potholes, and other deteriorations using AI-powered image analysis.

You can either upload an image of the road or use the live camera feed to detect issues in real time.

How Road Damage Detection Works
  • Upload a road image or use the live camera feed.
  • Our AI model analyzes the image and identifies damages like cracks, potholes, and other surface issues.
  • The system highlights the damaged areas with bounding boxes and provides a confidence percentage for each type.
  • Additionally, it classifies the road condition into Good,
  • These insights can help city planners, maintenance teams, and engineers take timely action.
